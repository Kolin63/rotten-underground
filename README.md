<h1>Rotten&nbsp;Underground</h1>
<p>Johnny's working in the Graywater Subway System, like he has been for the past decade. But this time, something goes horribly wrong. You've got to escape, explore the system, meet people, and uncover the deepest, darkest mystery under the surface of the city, the rat infestation.&nbsp;</p>
<h1>Gameplay</h1>
<p>Walk around, fight rats, and defeat the boss. Left mouse button to attack, right mouse button to switch weapons, WASD/Arrow Keys to move.</p>
